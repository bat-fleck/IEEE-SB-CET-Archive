Paper 1:

Electroculographic Technique of Interactive Communication for Patients with Motor Disability and Speech Impairment

Team Members:George Sebastian, Gopika G Kumar, Varsha Vinod , Visakh K

Paper Presentation 2 nd ICICT 2017, Coimbatore

Pattern Formation in Swarm Robotic Systems

Team Members Vaishnav J, Adithya B Uday, Thomson Poulose

Objective Development of an algorithm for formation of a word given as input, by 9 identical robots in a finite amount of time. Implementation of the algorithm onto the robots by means of wireless communication and feedback from rotary encoders. Implementation of collision avoidance in multi-robotic systems.

Paper Accepted for GHTC 2017 : Dynamic Traffic Light system for Unhindered Passing of High Priority Vehicles

Objective : Implementation of a low cost wireless smart traffic system with decentralised processing power using a mesh like communication network between traffic lights. System is able to provide High Priority Vehicles, like ambulances, a free lane, via a Green Wave of traffic lights, to their destination.
