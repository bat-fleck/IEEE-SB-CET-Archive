HUB ONE STAC 2011

by laxmi|Published July 10, 2011

STAC in Hub 1 will start off from July 16th,Saturday. The first STAC is going to be hosted by College of Engineering, Kottarakara on the topic Multi-core computing

Multicore computing is, at its most basic, the issue of how to fit multiple processing cores onto a single chip to produce a functional and efficient whole. There are many reasons that this is a valuable goal: the computer industry has moved towards multiple cores for increased performance, power efficiency and compute capacity in recent years.

In this competitive world, success gets more shining through effective points. Turn those points in your life to actions and get enlightened.

 

 

Speakers :-

Mr Veenus A V, 16 year of industrial experience in engineering solutions for international clients from 2005 onwards has worked in many flavors of Graphics Processing Unit (GPU) Still working as a consultant in the same field.

Mr. JIJO CHITTADY one of the directors KENSHU foundation conducted over 3000 trainings in softskill management an now he is conducting many popular television programs

Date: 16th July,2011

Venue: Seminar Hall CEK

Time: 9am -5pm

For IEEE members: Rs. 120

Non- IEEE : Rs. 150
