About IEEE SB CET

OUR MISSION

We at IEEE SB CET aim to provide a technical home, a place where each student member can develop and hone skills that will earmark them for prominent positions in their careers. Through volunteering in the variety of workshops and events that we host, speaking in and attending chapter sessions, our members get to explore new realms of interest and adapt to different situations enabling them to easily fit into any technical or professional role, even after their student life. We aim at providing an ideal platform for collaboration and interaction between students and professionals, facilitating the exchange of new ideas and fostering the growth of novel innovations.

DARREL CHONG AWARD 2015

IEEE SB CET received the most prestigious DARREL CHONG AWARD (Gold Medal) for conducting AKSC 2014

SPark

Spark is an outreach program conducted by IEEE SB CET for school students.It gives
students a glance into the world of Engineering, providing them a conception about the
various disciplines, the marvels and challenges of engineering and so on.The event includes workshop visits,sessions on latest technological advancements and so on.The previous iteration of the event saw participation from over ten schools and receiving great feedback and response from students and teachers alike.

AGM IEEE SB CET

During the SB Annual General Body Meeting reports of the previous year’s activities  and plans ,agendas for the ensuing year are discussed. This is followed by the IEEE SB CET Chair giving a message and the new execom members taking charge.

WORKSHOPS

Workshops are an integral part of IEEE SB CET’s routine functioning. Each of our societies
actively conduct workshops on a wide spectrum of topics, receiving participation from all
quarters of students. Our workshops also provide a platform for networking opportunities
between students of different branches, between batches and on several occasions, even
between colleges.

DAILY AND WEEKLY SESSIONS

Daily and Weekly Sessions of the various societies of our student branch are regularly
attended by enthusiastic students. These sessions foster assimilation of knowledge accrued from various sources and encourage doubts and innovative thinking. The sessions are conducted on a range of topics with some being standalone ones and others being session series.

IEEE DAY

IEEE Day Celebrations is never confined to a single day at IEEE SB CET. Rather, we
extensively dedicate an entire week to IEEE related events and activities. Workshops, Talk
sessions and a wide variety of other events are conducted with great zeal.

AURA

Aura is intended as the first IEEE event that all new IEEE student members from CET SB
attend.  The event provides an introduction to new members about IEEE, its scope, range,
opportunities and vision. It is also meant as an ice-breaker for all the IEEE freshers in college so that they may become comfortable with each other and be able to work together seamlessly in the future.
