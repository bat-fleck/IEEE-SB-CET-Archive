Power and energy Society

To be the leading provider of scientific and engineering information on electric power and energy for the betterment of society, and the preferred professional development source for our members
Mission

IEEE power and Eneygy society

The Power & Energy Society(PES)provides the world’s largest forum for sharing the latest in technological development in the electric power industry, for developing standards that guide the development and construction of equipment and systems, and for educating members of the industry and the general public.

Benefits of PES membership

Benefits of pes

Free subscription to PES award-winning Power & Energy magazine

Free access to over 1000 technical reports, tutorials, videos and presentations on the PES Resource Center

Discounts to technical tutorials

Free digital subscription to Electrification magazine

Discounts at dozens of PES sponsored or co-sponsored conferences and meetings in your area and around the world

Access to dozens of live and archived technical and professional development webinars

About pes chapter of Sb cet

Members of Power and Energy Society are leaders in this field, and they- and their employers - derive substantial benefits from involvement with this unique and outstanding association. Power and Energy Society, IEEE SB CET began functioning on 19th August 2017. We hope to positively augment the current existential energy race in every possible way to not only create new avenues in energy research but to bring them to the doorstep of the common man.
