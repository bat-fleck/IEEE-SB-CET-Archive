Workshop on Swarm Robotics Session -2(openCV)

September 27, 2017 @ 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Event Navigation

  • « SWARM ROBOTICS SERIES SESSION 1:PYTHON
  • ROS Workshop »

Registrations are closed for this event

RAS CET Chapter is conducting a series of workshops on swarm robotics which has applications  in several tasks that demand miniaturization, like distributed sensing tasks in micro machinery or the human body, mining tasks or agricultural foraging tasks,target searching, military applications, etc.

The workshop series will have a number of sessions on various topics covering among others Python Programming, Open CV, Rotary Encoders and the NodeMCU firmware. There will also be a CAD and Solidworks workshop. All participants may attend the sessions and receive a NodeMCU module.
After the initial sessions, there will be a screening based on a task involving Python and Image Processing after which 9 selected teams will attend the final two day workshop where the bots are made and swarm algorithm implemented.
